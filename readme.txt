== Why use SMTP for Wordpress website == 

Optimizing email communication on a WordPress website requires the use of SMTP (Simple Mail Transfer Protocol). By using orange SMTP dedicated mail servers,SMTP improves email delivery and lowers the risk of emails being flagged as spam. 
In addition, because SMTP servers follow the correct email sending protocols, emails are less likely to be tagged by providers, 
which helps prevent spam filters. 

In addition, SMTP provides authentication for increased security. guarantees dependability when processing high email volumes. Setting up WordPress to connect to an SMTP server improves the efficacy of email notifications and website communications overall. This is typically easily accomplished with plugins such as Orange SMTP.

== SMTP Mailer ==

Orange SMTP plugin contains a variety of SMTP mailers from which you can select the one that best suits your needs.

#### Gmail / Google Workspace

Google's Gmail SMTP (Simple Mail Transfer Protocol) service allows users to send emails from their Gmail accounts using third-party email clients or applications. SMTP is required for individuals who want to utilize Gmail as the outgoing mail server for platforms such as WordPress or other content management systems.

There is no need for technical knowledge. Just required collect the Gmail APP password. 

Read our <a href="https://orangesmtp.com/gmail-mailer/">Gmail Documentation</a> for details.

#### Mailgun

Mailgun is a become well-known email service provider that provides a range of services to help organizations and developers manage and enhance their email conversations.Orange SMTP leverages Free Mailgun SMTP to send your WordPress emails reliably and efficiently. Mailgun offers a 3-month free trial period during which you can send 5000 emails every month.

Read our <a href="https://orangesmtp.com/mailgun/"> Mailgun Documentation</a> for details.

### Other SMTP

You may use our Other SMTP mailer to send your WordPress emails to any SMTP server.You can use any one smtp server following popular email services Outlook, Yahoo, Hotmail are all examples of email clients.
